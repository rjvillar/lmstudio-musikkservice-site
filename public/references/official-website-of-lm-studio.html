Music services with personal support

LM Studio & Musikkservice is located between Brumunddal and Moelv, and supplies everything within accordions, two-row accordions and music technical services. Since 2001, we have helped both enthusiasts and professionals with everything from purchases and service to recording and sound engineering. With direct import and low costs, we offer both new and used instruments at the right prices, and guarantee personal follow-up – regardless of the project.

Right price - right quality

Direct import, low costs and warranty on used instruments.

Everything in one place

Everything from sales and service to courses, studio and sound engineering - with us you get everything under one roof.

Personal follow-up

We adapt our services to your wishes and needs.

We can help you with

Sale of accordions and two-stringed instruments

New and used accordions and two-row accordions - with guidance, customization and a 3-month warranty on used models. We help you find the instrument that suits your needs.

Workshop and service

Fully equipped workshop for repair, tuning and maintenance of all types of accordions and two-stringed instruments. Also performs MIDI and digital assembly upon request.

Courses and teaching

Accordion and double bass lessons, for both beginners and advanced players. Lessons are tailored to level and goals, and support lessons can be booked if needed.

Music studio

Recording, mixing and mastering in our own studio. We help you from idea to finished recorded demo or album, and also offer copying and CD production.

Sound and PA services

Delivery of sound equipment and sound technical assistance for concerts, events and festivals. We adapt the solution to your event and budget.

Digitization and transfer

We preserve your music memories – digitizing cassettes, reel-to-reel tapes and LPs into modern file formats.

Sheet music and accessories

Sheet music and other accessories are available upon request – please contact us if there is anything you are missing.

Factory fitted MIDI 

We offer factory-assembled MIDI, fully installed and tested directly from the manufacturer. This ensures high quality, quick installation and a reliable solution tailored to your needs.

Who are we?

LM Studio & Musikkservice is run by Geir-Harry Haugland, an experienced accordionist and music enthusiast with a solid background in instrument service and sound engineering. The business is built on a passion for music, personal service and reliable cooperation. We welcome visits by appointment, and offer flexible opening hours all year round.
